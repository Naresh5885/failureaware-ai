===================================================================
SECTION 12.0: ONCOLOGY CARE & CHEMOTHERAPY COVERAGE POLICY
===================================================================

12.1 Chemotherapy Infusion Coverage:
Outpatient chemotherapy infusions are covered at 90% up to a maximum annual payout limit of $25,000.00 per policy year.
Prior Authorization from the Oncology Medical Board is strictly required.

12.2 Radiation Therapy:
Stereotactic body radiation therapy (SBRT) is covered up to $12,000.00 per course of treatment.

12.3 Specialty Oncology Formularies:
Keytruda (Pembrolizumab): Tier 3 Specialty Drug, $100 Copay per infusion. Prior Auth Required.
Opdivo (Nivolumab): Tier 3 Specialty Drug, $100 Copay per infusion. Prior Auth Required.

12.4 Experimental Cancer Treatments:
Unapproved clinical trials and experimental immunotherapy treatments are excluded from coverage (0% payout).
