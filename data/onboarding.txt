Employee Onboarding Policy

Welcome to the company. This document outlines what new employees can expect during onboarding.

Probation Period: All new employees undergo a probation period of 6 months. During this time, performance will be reviewed monthly. Confirmation of employment is subject to satisfactory performance.

Joining Formalities: New employees must submit all required documents including educational certificates, previous employment letters, identity proof, address proof, and PAN card within 7 days of joining.

Induction Program: A structured induction program runs for the first 2 weeks. It covers company culture, policies, department introductions, and tool access setup.

System Access: IT accounts, email, and internal tools will be provisioned within 24 hours of joining. Employees must complete the mandatory security training within the first week.

Buddy Program: Each new employee is assigned a buddy from their team to help with informal questions and settling in.

ID Card and Access: Physical ID cards and office access cards are issued on the first day. Loss of ID card must be reported to HR and security immediately.

Dress Code: Business casual is the standard dress code from Monday to Thursday. Fridays are casual dress days.

Training and Development: Employees are encouraged to complete at least 40 hours of learning per year through the company's L&D platform.
