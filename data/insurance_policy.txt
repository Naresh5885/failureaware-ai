# US Comprehensive Health & Emergency Insurance Policy Terms (2026 Edition)

## Section 1: General Coverage & Deductibles
1.1 Annual Individual Deductible: $1,000. Family Deductible: $2,500.
1.2 Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $5,000 per individual per calendar year.
1.3 In-network services are covered at 80% after meeting the annual deductible. Out-of-network services are covered at 50%.

## Section 2: Dental & Vision Coverage Limits
2.1 Routine Dental Cleanings: 100% covered, up to 2 cleanings per year.
2.2 Major Dental Treatments (e.g., Root Canals, Crowns, Orthodontics): Maximum payout cap of $500 per procedure, with a maximum annual dental benefit limit of $1,500 per member.
2.3 Vision & Eyewear: Annual allowance of $200 for prescription glasses or contact lenses. Routine eye exam covered up to $100 per year.

## Section 3: Hospitalization & Emergency Room Claims
3.1 Emergency Room (ER) Visits: Covered with a $250 copay per visit. Copay is waived if the member is admitted to the hospital within 24 hours.
3.2 Inpatient Hospital Stay: Requires prior authorization. Maximum covered room rate is $1,200 per day up to 30 continuous days per stay.
3.3 Prescription Medications: Tier 1 Generic ($15 copay), Tier 2 Preferred Brand ($40 copay), Tier 3 Non-Preferred Specialty ($80 copay).

## Section 4: Pre-Existing Conditions & Exclusions
4.1 Pre-existing conditions are fully covered after a 6-month continuous enrollment waiting period.
4.2 Experimental or unapproved medical treatments are strictly non-covered and ineligible for reimbursement.
4.3 Cosmetic surgeries are excluded unless medically necessary following an accident or trauma reconstruction.
