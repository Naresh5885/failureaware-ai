# Comprehensive Surgical & Hospitalization Insurance Guidelines (2026)

## Section 5: Inpatient & Outpatient Surgical Limits
5.1 Outpatient Surgical Procedures (e.g. Cataract surgery, Knee Arthroscopy, Appendectomy): Covered at 80% after deductible, up to a maximum payout of $3,500 per procedure.
5.2 Major Inpatient Surgery (e.g. Open Heart Surgery, Spinal Fusion, Joint Replacement): Requires pre-admission certification 72 hours prior to admission. Covered up to $25,000 lifetime benefit cap per individual member.
5.3 Intensive Care Unit (ICU) Room Rates: Covered up to $2,500 per day for a maximum of 14 continuous days.

## Section 6: Emergency Ambulance & Transportation
6.1 Ground Ambulance Services: Covered up to $800 per emergency trip to the nearest qualified facility.
6.2 Air Ambulance Services: Covered up to $5,000 per emergency evacuation when medically necessary and life-threatening.
6.3 Non-emergency medical transport is strictly non-covered.

## Section 7: Fraud Prevention & Audit Rules
7.1 Multiple claim submissions for the same service date by the same provider will trigger an automatic fraud risk flag.
7.2 Claims submitted within 30 days of initial policy enrollment are subjected to pre-existing condition audit.
