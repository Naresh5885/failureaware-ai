Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to the following types of leave per calendar year:

Casual Leave: 12 days per year. Casual leave can be availed for short-term personal needs. It cannot be carried forward to the next year.

Sick Leave: 10 days per year. Sick leave requires a medical certificate for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

Earned Leave / Annual Leave: 18 days per year. Earned leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 30 days.

Maternity Leave: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave as per statutory guidelines.

Paternity Leave: Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paternity leave within 6 months of the child's birth.

Public Holidays: Employees are entitled to all national public holidays declared by the government, typically 10-12 days per year.

Leave Application: All leave must be applied for in advance through the HR portal, except for emergency sick leave which may be applied retroactively within 2 working days.

Probation Period: Employees on probation (first 6 months) are entitled to 1 casual leave per month. Earned leave accrual begins after probation confirmation.
