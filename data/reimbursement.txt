Reimbursement Policy

This policy covers the reimbursement of expenses incurred by employees in the course of official duties.

Travel Reimbursement: Employees traveling for official purposes will be reimbursed for train, bus, or economy-class air travel. Travel must be pre-approved by the reporting manager and HR.

Local Conveyance: Employees using personal vehicles for official local travel will be reimbursed at the rate of Rs. 5 per km for two-wheelers and Rs. 8 per km for four-wheelers.

Accommodation: For outstation travel, accommodation reimbursement is capped based on employee grade: up to Rs. 3,000/night for junior staff, Rs. 5,000/night for mid-level, and Rs. 8,000/night for senior staff.

Meal Allowance: A daily meal allowance of Rs. 500 per day is provided during official travel. Receipts must be submitted for claims above Rs. 250.

Mobile and Internet: Employees in designated roles receive a monthly mobile and internet reimbursement of up to Rs. 1,000 upon submission of bills.

Medical Reimbursement: Employees are eligible for medical reimbursement of up to Rs. 15,000 per year for self and dependents. Claims must be submitted with original prescriptions and bills.

Claim Process: All reimbursement claims must be submitted through the expense portal within 30 days of incurring the expense. Claims submitted after 30 days may not be processed.

Receipts: Original receipts or GST invoices are mandatory for all claims above Rs. 100.
